About YAF & Otona Disney

Disney Japan targets a very specific population group, the YAF (prounounced yahãƒ»foo) also known as Young Adult Females. The accomplishments and successes achieved by Disney Japan can be attributed to the careful user targeting.

The YAF group is designated as females aged 18-35, who are interested in fashion, current trends, and most importantly, Disney. We brand our products as Otona Disney which roughly translates to a Disney brand that can be enjoyed by adults, rather than children. Furthermore, we segment our user group into Core Fans, and Non-Rejectors.

The Core Fan is exactly what she sounds like: a hard core Disney fans. These users are obsessed about Disney and they aren't afraid to show their appreciation for the brand via merchandising. The Core Fan has probably watched every Disney movie (some more than once) and is aware of all the newest and latest updates from Disney. These users also tend enjoy decorating all aspects of their life with Disney.

While the Non-Rejector enjoys the Disney brand, they aren't as gung ho about it as the Core Fan. The Non-Rejector may even feel embarassed to openly display their brand appreciation. These users have probably watched most of the Disney movies, but might not necessarily be marking their calendars for releases marked three years in the future. Non-rejectors enjoy Disney products but don't use it to define their style.
